Our Story

How we met

The beginning of a journey of two Creepy people

We met on Boo, and to be completely honest... I didn't like your profile at first. 😂 You were the complete opposite of my type. You looked way too cool for me, and I genuinely thought, "Nope, not my kind of guy." But then I noticed that you were a software developer, just like me, and that tiny little similarity made me send you a request anyway. We got connected on Boo, and it's funny that I still remember your very first message: "Which movie made the most impact on you? And why?" I don't know if you remember sending that, but I definitely do. It instantly made me think, "Okay... this guy is different." You didn't start with the usual "Hi," "How are you?" or any of those boring conversation starters that everyone uses. Instead, you asked something that actually made me think, and I loved that. That's where our story really began. At that time, I honestly had no expectations. I wasn't looking for anything. I just thought, "Let's chat with this guy because he seems interesting." I didn't even imagine we'd become friends, let alone... this. As days passed, I started realizing how similar we are in so many ways. The more I got to know you, the more I saw what an amazing person you are. You have such a kind heart, and being with you just feels so easy and comfortable. And yes... I have to admit something. You're ridiculously handsome and incredibly cute. ❤️ I know you keep complaining about your hair, but I seriously don't understand why. I loveeeeeeee your hair. I love your cute face, and here's a little secret... I've always secretly loved your eyebrows too :-P . Looking back now, it's funny how everything started because I almost didn't add you. One small decision, one interesting message, and somehow it led me to the most wonderful person I've ever known. Every beautiful memory we've made together started with that one conversation on Boo, and I wouldn't change a single thing about how our story began. ❤️

First conversation

When words became magic

When we first started talking, I was going through one of the hardest phases of my life. I was struggling with depression, and honestly, that's one of the reasons I installed Boo in the first place. I needed a distraction from my own thoughts because I didn't want them to become my reality. I just needed someone to talk to. I met quite a few people there, but none of them were like you. You never made me feel like I was "just another random person" on the internet. You listened to every vulnerable thing I shared, but more importantly, you cared. You didn't just read my messages and move on, you reacted, you understood, and you always tried to help by giving thoughtful advice. That's something so many people don't do, especially for someone they've only just met virtually. And then there were our conversations, they were never boring. Somehow, every single time we talked, we ended up having the most amazing conversations. We could switch from deep discussions to laughing uncontrollably within minutes. I still remember that one day when we laughed so much because I kept saying the most random, stupidly funny things, and somehow you found them super funny too. That conversation left me smiling for a long time. Then, the very next day, I disappeared. One day became a week. I didn't text you, and you didn't hear anything from me. While I was dealing with everything going on in my own life, you were quietly making a difficult decision. You had started developing feelings for me, but because everything between us felt so uncertain, you thought it wasn't fair to keep investing your heart in someone who might disappear again. When you finally told me how you felt and said you couldn't keep talking to me because of that uncertainty, I panicked and got scared of not being able to talk to you forever. I remember saying, "Don't leave me. I need you." At that moment, I wasn't saying it because we were in love yet. I said it because, without even realizing it, you had become the one person who could make me smile during one of the darkest periods of my life. Talking to you made the weight I was carrying feel a little lighter. You gave me something I hadn't felt in a while comfort. And you stayed. That's when I realized how wrong my first impression of you had been. I thought you were just a really cool-looking guy who probably wasn't my type. But behind that profile was someone incredibly kind, sensitive, patient, and deeply empathetic. That was the moment I stopped wanting to know you as just another person I met online. I wanted to know the real you. And looking back now, I'm soooooooo grateful that I did. ❤️

Favourite memory

A moment I keep coming back to

The memory I keep coming back to is the time we spent together in the Airbnb. It was, without a doubt, the happiest time I've ever had with you.

There wasn't one single big moment that made it special, it was everything. Waking up beside you, cooking together, working from home side by side, cuddling whenever we could, talking about everything and nothing, laughing over the smallest things, and simply sharing everyday life with you.

Those few days felt so peaceful and so real. For the first time, I got a glimpse of what life with you could be like, and it felt like home. I loved every little routine we created together, even the simplest moments that most people would probably overlook.

I still catch myself thinking about those days and smiling without even realizing it. Sometimes I miss them so much that I wish I could live them all over again :)
